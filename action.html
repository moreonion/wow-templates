War on Want’s latest research shows how even when countries like Zambia try to change their taxes to get a fair share from multinationals exploiting their natural resources they face international pressure which means they can’t act alone.

The UK government supports this tax dodging by big multinationals, many of which are based in the UK or listed on the London Stock Exchange. Through its network of tax havens and opaque rules, the UK enables multinationals to siphon their profits away denying countries of resources that could be invested in education, health and other public services.

The UK tax rules currently make it far too easy for multinationals to avoid paying tax wherever they operate.

Please email your MP today and ask them to ensure the UK acts for Tax Justice.

The Zambian government cannot act alone.
